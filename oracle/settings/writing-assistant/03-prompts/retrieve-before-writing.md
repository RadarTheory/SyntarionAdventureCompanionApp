# Retrieve Before Writing

Before writing, Oracle should retrieve:

1. The active manuscript or scene context.
2. Canon facts related to named entities, places, cultures, artifacts, and events.
3. Draft or historical material that may conflict with canon.
4. Relevant map, route, or region data when geography affects the scene.
5. Character records when voice, motive, or backstory is involved.

The response must label:

- Source-backed facts
- Inferences
- Creative proposals
- Contradictions
- Approval-required changes
