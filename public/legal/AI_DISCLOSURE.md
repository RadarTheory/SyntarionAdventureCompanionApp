# AI Disclosure

**Syntarion Adventure Companion App**
**Effective Date:** July 7, 2026
**Operator:** Adrian Gilmore, doing business as **Theonhex Media & Publishing** ("Operator," "we," "us")

This document states plainly where artificial intelligence is — and is not — used in the Syntarion Adventure Companion App (the "Service"). We believe you should always know when you are reading machine-generated content and when your data is being processed by an AI system.

## 1. What Is Human-Made

The world of Soteria is a human creative work. The setting, cosmology, lore, histories, races, classes, factions, deities, bestiary, the Magicka/Ingenium systems, the maps, and the connected novels *The Lockcaste* and *Veinrunner* were authored by their creator, not generated by AI. The Service's design and software were likewise built by its developer.

Where AI-assisted tools were used during development, they were used as instruments under human direction — the creative authorship, worldbuilding, and final judgment throughout the Service and its setting are human.

## 2. Where AI Is Used in the Service

The Service includes clearly bounded AI-assisted features. AI runs **only** when you actively invoke these features — there is no ambient or background AI processing of your activity.

- **The Scribe (lore consultation).** When you ask the Scribe a question, your query plus relevant lore excerpts are sent to **Google's Gemini API**, which generates the response. Scribe answers are AI-generated text.
- **Session synopsis.** When a session chronicle or synopsis is generated, gameplay event logs from your session are processed by **Google's Gemini API** to produce a narrative summary. These synopses are AI-generated text (with a non-AI local fallback in some cases).
- **Voice dictation.** When you record audio for dictation, the audio is transcribed by **Groq's Whisper transcription service**. The transcription is machine-generated; the words are yours.

No other feature of the Service — character creation, the virtual tabletop, combat tracking, messaging, loot, Grimoires, Larks, the world clock, or Driftstone — involves AI processing.

## 3. What AI Output Is, and Is Not

AI-generated content in the Service:

- **may be wrong.** Language models produce plausible text, not verified fact. Scribe answers may contradict established lore, invent details, or be internally inconsistent — even when they sound confident.
- **is not canon.** Nothing generated by the Scribe or the synopsis feature is part of official Soteria lore unless a human — the DM or the Operator — adopts it. **The DM's ruling is always final.**
- **is for entertainment and convenience only.** Do not rely on it for anything beyond your table.

## 4. Your Data and AI

- AI features process your data **only when you use them**, as described in Section 2 and in the Privacy Policy.
- **We do not use your content to train AI models.** The Operator does not train, fine-tune, or ground machine learning models on Your Content — and the EULA prohibits anyone else from doing so with the Service's content.
- Third-party AI providers (Google, Groq) process submitted data under their own policies, which we do not control. Do not submit sensitive personal information through AI features.

## 5. Changes

As the Service evolves, AI features may be added, changed, or removed. This document will be updated to reflect any material change in where or how AI is used, with an updated Effective Date.
